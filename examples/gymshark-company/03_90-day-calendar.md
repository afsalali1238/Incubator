# 90-Day Build Calendar — Gymshark

## Weeks 1–2 — Validate (Idea stage)
**Owner:** Community Manager
- DM 50 fitness YouTubers offering free, custom-tailored apparel.
- Gather size and address data for the 10 who say yes.
**Exit:** 10 committed influencers willing to receive product.

## Weeks 3–6 — Build the core product (MVP stage)
**Owner:** Apparel Designer
- Buy 50 blank stringer vests.
- Sew the tapers by hand in the garage.
- Screen-print the shark logo onto the chest.
**Exit:** 10 packages mailed out to the influencers.

## Weeks 7–10 — Iterate toward evidence (MVP → Launch)
**Owner:** Community Manager
- Monitor YouTube uploads. Engage heavily in the comment sections when the shirts appear on camera.
- Funnel viewers to a landing page to capture emails.
**Exit:** 500 emails on the waitlist.

## Weeks 11–12 — Launch readiness (Launch stage)
**Owner:** E-commerce Ops Lead
- Open the Shopify store for a limited 24-hour "drop".
- Print shipping labels.
**Exit:** Completely sold out of the first 200-unit batch.
