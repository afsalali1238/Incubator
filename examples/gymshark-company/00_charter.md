# Gymshark — Company Charter

**Project:** Gymshark. A D2C fitness apparel brand focused strictly on the aesthetic bodybuilding and weightlifting community, starting with screen-printed stringer vests tailored for a tapered physique.
**CEO Verdict:** BUILD. The incumbent brands (Nike, Under Armour) are too broad and their fits do not cater to modern gym aesthetics.
**Riskiest Assumption:** Influencers (fitness YouTubers) will wear free clothes in their videos, and their audiences will trust them enough to buy from an unknown brand website.
**The Wedge:** Screen-printing 100 longer, tighter-fitting stringer vests in a garage and sending them for free to the top 10 fitness YouTubers.
**The Scope Fence:** We are NOT manufacturing custom fabrics or technical activewear yet. We are buying blank garments and screen-printing them.

## Org at a Glance
1. **Community / Influencer Manager** (Idea/MVP) — to build relationships with YouTubers.
2. **Apparel Designer / Screen Printer** (MVP) — to handle the physical garment printing.
3. **E-commerce Ops Lead** (Launch) — to manage Shopify, fulfillment, and customer service.

## Next Action
Identify the top 10 fitness YouTubers whose aesthetic matches the brand, and find their PO Box or contact email to send free samples.
