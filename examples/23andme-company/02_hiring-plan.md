# Hiring Plan — 23andMe

## The Org Chart

```
CEO (The Founder)
├── Clinical / Regulatory SME (MVP / FDA Compliance)
├── Geneticist / Data Scientist (MVP / The Algorithm)
└── Consumer Trust Designer (Launch / UX)
```

## The Hire Sequence

1. **Clinical / Regulatory SME** — Hired first because if we violate FDA regulations, the company goes to zero overnight.
2. **Geneticist / Data Scientist** — Hired second to take the raw SNP data from the lab and map it to actual, readable human traits.
3. **Consumer Trust Designer** — Hired third to design the physical spit-kit packaging and the web UI so that giving away your DNA feels like a premium, safe experience rather than a clinical procedure.

## The Roster

### Clinical / Regulatory SME
**De-risks:** Government shutdown and lab compliance.
**First win:** Securing a partnership with a CLIA-certified laboratory and defining the exact legal boundaries of what our reports can say.
**Skill/Brief:** [team/regulatory-sme.md](./team/regulatory-sme.md)
