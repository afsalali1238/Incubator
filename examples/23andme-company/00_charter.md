# 23andMe — Company Charter

**Project:** 23andMe. A direct-to-consumer DNA testing kit that empowers individuals to access, understand, and benefit from the human genome.
**CEO Verdict:** BUILD (Pending Regulatory Review). The technology to sequence DNA is dropping exponentially in price, opening up a massive consumer market previously gatekept by doctors.
**Riskiest Assumption:** Consumers are willing to spit in a tube and mail their DNA to a startup, and the FDA won't immediately shut the operation down for practicing medicine without a license.
**The Wedge:** Launching strictly as an "ancestry and traits" service to bypass stringent medical diagnostic regulations while building the world's largest genetic database.
**The Scope Fence:** We are NOT providing medical diagnoses, prescribing treatments, or acting as a healthcare provider. We are an informational service.

## Org at a Glance
1. **Clinical / Regulatory SME** (Idea/MVP) — to navigate FDA compliance and CLIA lab certification.
2. **Geneticist / Data Scientist** (MVP) — to build the algorithms that map SNPs to ancestry and traits.
3. **Consumer Trust Designer** (Launch) — to make the spit-kit experience feel premium, safe, and entirely secure.

## Next Action
Hire a regulatory expert to determine the exact legal boundary between "recreational genetic information" and "medical device."
