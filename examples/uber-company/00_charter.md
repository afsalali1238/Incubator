# Uber — Company Charter

**Project:** UberCab. A dispatch service to let you order a black car on-demand via SMS from your smartphone.
**CEO Verdict:** BUILD. The taxi industry in San Francisco is fundamentally broken, highly regulated, and terrible for the consumer.
**Riskiest Assumption:** Normal people are willing to get into a stranger's car, and drivers are willing to use their own personal phones to receive dispatch orders.
**The Wedge:** Starting exclusively with licensed black car / limo drivers who have downtime between corporate jobs, operating only in San Francisco, and dispatching via SMS.
**The Scope Fence:** We are NOT building the "UberX" peer-to-peer model yet. We are strictly acting as a dispatch layer for existing licensed black car drivers.

## Org at a Glance
1. **City General Manager / Ops Lead** (Idea/MVP) — to physically recruit drivers and handle local transport licensing.
2. **Dispatch / Backend Engineer** (MVP) — to build the SMS-to-driver routing logic.
3. **Driver Onboarding Specialist** (Launch) — to hand out iPhones to drivers who don't have them.

## Next Action
Find 5 licensed black car drivers in SF who have idle time during the day and convince them to accept an SMS from us when we have a passenger.
