# 90-Day Build Calendar — Uber

## Weeks 1–2 — Validate (Idea stage)
**Owner:** City General Manager
- Visit black car staging areas (SFO airport, downtown hotels).
- Pitch drivers on accepting dispatch via SMS during their idle time.
**Exit:** 5 licensed drivers agree to try it and give us their phone numbers.

## Weeks 3–6 — Build the core product (MVP stage)
**Owner:** Dispatch Engineer
- Build a simple Twilio-based backend to receive an SMS from a rider and forward it to the 5 drivers.
- The first driver to reply "ACCEPT" gets the ride.
**Exit:** The system successfully routes 10 test rides without crashing.

## Weeks 7–10 — Iterate toward evidence (MVP → Launch)
**Owner:** City General Manager
- Give the SMS dispatch number to 50 tech founder friends in SF.
- Monitor every single dispatch manually. Call the driver if they don't respond.
**Exit:** 50 completed rides with an average wait time under 10 minutes.

## Weeks 11–12 — Launch readiness (Launch stage)
**Owner:** Dispatch Engineer & Ops Lead
- Build the actual iOS rider app to replace SMS (push a button, see the car on a map).
- Procure 20 iPhones to hand to new drivers so they can run the driver app.
**Exit:** The iOS app is live in the App Store and 20 drivers are active in SF.
