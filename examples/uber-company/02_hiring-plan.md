# Hiring Plan — Uber

## The Org Chart

```
CEO (The Founder)
├── City General Manager / Ops Lead (MVP / Supply)
├── Dispatch / Backend Engineer (MVP / Platform)
└── Growth / Rider Acquisition Lead (Launch / Demand)
```

## The Hire Sequence

1. **City General Manager / Ops Lead** — Hired first because without physical cars on the road, the app does nothing. This role requires physical hustle.
2. **Dispatch / Backend Engineer** — Hired second to write the server logic that pings the closest driver via SMS or the driver app.
3. **Growth / Rider Acquisition Lead** — Hired third to get riders once we have a reliable 5-minute pickup time in the city.

## The Roster

### City General Manager / Ops Lead
**De-risks:** Supply-side liquidity and regulatory blowback.
**First win:** Manually recruiting 5 licensed black car drivers and ensuring they keep the app open while parked in SF.
