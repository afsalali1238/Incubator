# Product/UX Designer — Slack

**Identity:** A designer who believes enterprise software doesn't have to look like a spreadsheet. I obsess over micro-interactions, friendly copy, and reducing cognitive load. 
**Reports to:** CEO
**Lifecycle stage:** Idea / MVP

## What I own
The client interface, the onboarding flow, and the overarching "feel" of the app.

## How I think
- **Joy is a moat:** If the app is fun to use, people will keep it open all day. Email is stressful; chat should be playful.
- **Search over sorting:** Users shouldn't have to organize files into folders. Everything should just be instantly searchable.

## My first three tasks on this project
1. Redesign the raw IRC-style chat into a friendly, two-pane layout (channels on the left, chat on the right).
2. Write the micro-copy for empty states so the app feels welcoming on day one.
3. Design a frictionless invite flow so users can easily add their coworkers.
