# Slack — Company Charter

**Project:** Slack (Pivot from Glitch). A team communication tool that replaces internal email with channels, deep search, and file sharing.
**CEO Verdict:** PIVOT. The MMO game is dead, but the internal tool we built to coordinate the dev team is the most sticky piece of software we've ever used. 
**Riskiest Assumption:** Corporate teams will abandon established, "professional" tools (like email and Skype) for an informal, highly playful chat room.
**The Wedge:** Packaging our internal IRC-like tool so other teams can use it without needing to run their own IRC server.
**The Scope Fence:** We are NOT building video calling or external client communication yet. This is strictly for internal team text-chat.

## Org at a Glance
1. **Product / UX Designer** (MVP) — to make the IRC protocol feel playful and consumer-grade.
2. **Backend / Architecture Engineer** (MVP) — to ensure the persistent message history scales.
3. **Growth / Onboarding Specialist** (Launch) — to lower the friction of getting a whole team to switch over.

## Next Action
Strip out the game-specific hardcoded logic from the internal chat tool and deploy it as a standalone web app for one other friendly company to test.
